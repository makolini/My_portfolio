About Me

I am a data science student with a background in Psychology (Brain and Cognition) currently pursuing my Master's in Behavioural Data Science at the University of Amsterdam. My expertise combines statistical analysis, machine learning, and understanding human behavior. I work on various data science projects, both on Kaggle and with real clients. I enjoy combining technical skills with real-world applications, especially where psychology meets data science.

My main interests are:

  • Mobile app development
  • Data visualization and analysis in R
  • Applying ML to cognitive and behavioral data
  • Testing out latest AI models and tools
